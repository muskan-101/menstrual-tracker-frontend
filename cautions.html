Health Precautions For PCOS/PCOD

PCOS & PCOD Awareness

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) and Polycystic Ovarian Disease (PCOD) are common hormonal conditions among women. Knowing the signs early can help in maintaining good reproductive and overall health.

⚠️ Common Symptoms

  • Irregular periods or skipped cycles
  • Acne or oily skin
  • Weight gain or difficulty losing weight
  • Excessive hair growth (face, chest, back)
  • Thinning scalp hair
  • Mood swings or fatigue

💡 Health Tips & Cautions

While PCOS/PCOD cannot be cured completely, lifestyle management helps a lot. Here’s what you can do:

  • Maintain a balanced diet rich in fiber and low in sugar
  • Exercise at least 30 minutes a day
  • Sleep 7–8 hours daily and manage stress
  • Avoid skipping meals or crash dieting
  • Consult a gynecologist if your cycles are irregular for more than 3 months
